Our Story

A Destination Worth the Drive

Aperitif Spritz & Bites is a quaint, eclectic lakefront dining experience housed in a restored 19th-century cottage on the Old Mandeville shore — the kind of place that calls you back. Born from the legacy of The Lakehouse and the vision of owner Cayman Sinclair, Aperitif blends the soul of old-school New Orleans hospitality with the relaxed elegance of a Parisian sidewalk bistro.

Opened in 2025 after a three-year hiatus following Hurricane Ida, Aperitif is a return and a celebration all at once. Come early, stay late. Bring your people or make new ones here. The lake is always waiting.

The Man Behind the Table

Meet Cayman Sinclair

A North Shore native and lifelong host, Cayman Sinclair has spent more than 30 years perfecting the art of making people feel welcome. His culinary philosophy was shaped by the golden era of Louisiana fine dining — the Commander's Palace years of Paul Prudhomme and Emeril Lagasse — and honed across decades of running restaurants, catering celebrity events, and feeding communities in the wake of disaster.

Sinclair opened his first restaurant, L.A. Grill, on Causeway Boulevard before Hurricane Katrina took it in 2005. Undeterred, he co-founded The Lakehouse in 2009 — one of the North Shore's most celebrated dining rooms — building it into a beloved institution before Hurricane Ida flooded the building and forced a closure in 2021. Rather than walk away, he returned. Aperitif Spritz & Bites is his answer.

"When it's what you've done for so long, it's hard to put down. It was kind of my identity."

— Cayman Sinclair
